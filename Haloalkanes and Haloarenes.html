Understanding Haloalkanes and Haloarenes

Haloalkanes and haloarenes are organic compounds in which halogen atoms (like fluorine, chlorine, bromine, or iodine) are attached to carbon atoms. They exhibit unique properties and reactivity due to the presence of halogens.
