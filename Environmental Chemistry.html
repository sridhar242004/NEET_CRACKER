Environmental Chemistry

Explore the chemical processes occurring in the environment and their impact on ecosystems and human health, essential for NEET preparation.

Introduction to Environmental Chemistry

Environmental chemistry focuses on the chemical changes in the environment, analyzing pollutants, their sources, and how they affect natural systems. This field helps us understand and mitigate human impact on the environment.

Water Pollution

Study of contaminants in water bodies, sources of pollution, and effects on aquatic life and human health.

Air Pollution

Understanding different air pollutants, their sources, and impacts on health, climate, and ecosystems.

Soil Pollution

Analysis of soil contaminants, waste management issues, and the effects of pollutants on plant life and agriculture.

Radioactive Pollution

Understanding the sources and dangers of radioactive contaminants and their environmental and biological impacts.

Green Chemistry and Sustainability

Green chemistry aims to design chemical products and processes that reduce or eliminate hazardous substances, promoting sustainability.

Principles of Green Chemistry

Core principles guiding sustainable chemical practices to minimize environmental impact.

Eco-friendly Reactions

Exploration of reactions and processes that minimize waste and use less toxic reagents.

Sustainable Manufacturing

Approaches in industry to reduce waste, energy usage, and pollution in chemical manufacturing.

Recycling and Waste Management

Methods for recycling and managing waste in ways that minimize environmental harm.
