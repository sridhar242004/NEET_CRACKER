Electromagnetic Waves

Explore the properties, types, and applications of electromagnetic waves essential for NEET preparation.

Introduction to Electromagnetic Waves

Electromagnetic waves are oscillations of electric and magnetic fields that travel through space. They are fundamental to various technologies and natural phenomena.

Nature of Electromagnetic Waves

Understanding the dual nature of electromagnetic waves as both particles and waves, including concepts like wavelength, frequency, and amplitude.

Types of Electromagnetic Waves

Exploring different types of electromagnetic waves, including radio waves, microwaves, infrared, visible light, ultraviolet, X-rays, and gamma rays.

Wave-Particle Duality

Examining how electromagnetic waves exhibit both wave-like and particle-like properties, leading to the concept of photons.

Propagation of Electromagnetic Waves

Understanding how electromagnetic waves propagate through different media, including vacuum and various materials.

Applications of Electromagnetic Waves

Electromagnetic waves play a crucial role in various applications across different fields, from communication to medicine.

Communication Systems

Utilization of radio waves and microwaves in wireless communication, including mobile phones and satellite transmissions.

Medical Imaging

Application of X-rays and other electromagnetic waves in medical diagnostics, including CT scans and MRIs.

Solar Energy

Harvesting solar energy using photovoltaic cells that convert visible light into electrical energy.

Radiation and Safety

Understanding the safety aspects and biological effects of exposure to various types of electromagnetic radiation.
