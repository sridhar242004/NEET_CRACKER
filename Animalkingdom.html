Understanding the Animal Kingdom

The animal kingdom is a vast and diverse group of organisms that share common characteristics, including multicellularity, heterotrophy, and complex behaviors. This section explores the major groups and their importance in ecosystems.

Understanding the hierarchical system of classifying animals based on shared characteristics, including phylum, class, order, family, genus, and species.

Analyzing the roles animals play in their ecosystems, including producers, consumers, decomposers, and their interactions with other species.
